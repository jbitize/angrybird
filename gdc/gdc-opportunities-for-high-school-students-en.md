## 3. Age-Based GDC Participation Timeline for Jaeho

### Official Policy

> "Due to safety concerns, no one under the age of 18 will be permitted to register for GDC."
> — [GDC Registration Policies](https://gdconf.com/registration-policies/)

**No one under 18 can register for GDC.**

### Jaeho's Participation Timeline

| GDC | Jaeho's Age | Grade | What's Possible |
|-----|-------------|-------|-----------------|
| **GDC 2027** (Mar 2027) | **17** | 11th | Student Day, IGF submission (parental consent required) |
| **GDC 2028** (Mar 2028) | **18** | 12th | **Full registration!** Academic Pass, GDC Play, GDC Pitch — all available |
| **GDC 2029** (Mar 2029) | **19** | College freshman | All programs available |

> Jaeho turns 18 on November 2, 2027, making **GDC 2028 (March 2028) the first GDC with full access**.

### The Only Exception: Student Day

GDC runs a supervised **Student Day** program each year.

- **Format**: Workshops led by partner organizations like Gameheads
- **Audience**: High school and college students (ages 15–25)
- **Content**: Game industry introduction, workshops, networking
- **Cost**: Free (register through partner organizations)
- **Reference**: [GDC 2025 Student Day Workshop](https://schedule.gdconf.com/session/gdc-2025-student-day-workshop-presented-by-gameheads/912171)

### Submitting as a 2-Person Team (Jaeho + Jonny)

**Not everyone on the team needs to be 18 to submit.** Per IGF rules:

- Team members under 18 can submit with **individual written parental/guardian consent**
- If one team member is 18+, that person can register as the lead — making the process smoother

**However, on-site attendance is a separate matter:**

| Scenario | On-Site Attendance |
|----------|-------------------|
| Both team members under 18 | **Cannot register.** Student Day only |
| One team member is 18+ | Only the 18+ member can register normally |
| If selected as IGF Finalist | 4 passes provided, but minors can **designate a proxy attendee** |

**Key distinction: Submission ≠ Attendance.**

> - **Submission**: Minors can submit with parental consent (both team members)
> - **Finalist selection**: You can become a finalist/winner without attending in person
> - **On-site exhibition**: If under 18, a parent can attend as a proxy, or participate through Student Day
>
> — [IGF Competition Rules](https://igf.com/igf-competition-rules/)

### IGF Finalist Exception

If selected as an IGF finalist, **4 Conference passes** are provided. Minors may attend with **written parental/guardian consent**. IGF rules explicitly state that those under 18 need parental consent, confirming that submission itself is allowed.

Additionally, IGF rules allow **Finalists who cannot attend to designate a proxy attendee**. This means even if Jaeho can't go in person, a parent could operate the booth on his behalf.

## 4. Eligible GDC Programs — Detailed Analysis

### 4-1. IGF (Independent Games Festival) Student Showcase ⭐ Top Priority

**Overview**: The world's most prestigious indie game competition. Running since 1998. Awards ceremony held during GDC.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Best Student Game Prize** | $2,000 |
| **Student Submission Fee** | $25 (fee waiver available) |
| **Submission Period (2026 ref)** | Sep 8 – Oct 10, 2025 |
| **Expected 2027 Submission** | Early Sep – Early Oct 2026 |
| **Finalist Announcement** | January |
| **Awards Ceremony** | During GDC |

#### Student Eligibility

> Developers who are "part-time or full-time college or **high school** students" at the time of submission qualify for the student category.

**Key: High school students CAN submit!**

#### Age Requirements

- 18 years or older, OR
- **Under 18 with written parental/guardian consent**

| Requirement | Status | Notes |
|-------------|:------:|-------|
| Student status | ✅ | Both are high school students |
| Playable build | ✅ | Demo exists, active user testing |

| Is it a "game"? | ⚠️ | Story-driven interactive VR experience — IGF accepts experimental works, but pre-inquiry recommended |
| Social impact | ✅✅ | Elderly/disabled focus — powerful differentiator |
| Non-student assets | ⚠️ Check needed | ACF coach involvement level may affect eligibility |

#### ⚠️ Important Note: The "Game" Eligibility Question

IGF officially accepts "games." Jaeho's project self-identifies as a "VR experience," placing it on the boundary. However:

**Factors supporting eligibility:**
- IGF's **Nuovo Award** honors work that "makes the jurors think differently about games as a medium" — non-traditional works welcome
- IGF has historically selected VR experiences, interactive art, and experimental works as Finalists
- Story-based narrative + user interaction = can be positioned as "interactive narrative VR game"

**Recommended Actions:**
1. Email IGF before submission to describe the project and confirm eligibility
2. Position as "interactive narrative VR game" when submitting
3. Emphasize social impact + Conrad Challenge semi-finalist credentials

#### Grand Prize

#### What to Prepare

1. Playable, polished Meta Quest build
2. Game description, screenshots, trailer
3. Build accessible to judges (APK or App Lab link)
4. Parental consent forms (both Jaeho & Jonny, if under 18)

## 5. Ineligible GDC Programs

### alt.ctrl.GDC — Not Applicable

Requires custom/alternative hardware controllers. Meta Quest is standard VR hardware, so **this project does not qualify**. Not eligible unless a custom physical controller is built.

### GDC Play — Under 18 Not Allowed (Available from GDC 2028)

Indie developer exhibition program. Connects developers with investors/publishers. Available for Jaeho starting GDC 2028 when he turns 18.

### GDC Pitch — Under 18 Not Allowed + High Cost (Consider from GDC 2028)

Live pitching competition. Requires Game Changer Pass ($2,499). A powerful opportunity after GDC 2028 when the VR app is officially launched.

## 7. Tips for Maximizing the GDC Experience

### Before Attending

1. **Prepare a 30-second elevator pitch**: "We're building a story-driven VR experience that helps elderly and disabled individuals lead active lives. We're testing it at actual senior living facilities and were Conrad Challenge semi-finalists."
2. **Print business cards**: Name, contact info, portfolio URL, project page link
3. **Have the demo ready**: A build playable on Meta Quest on the spot
4. **Review the schedule**: Bookmark sessions of interest in the GDC app
5. **Wear comfortable shoes**: You'll be walking all day

### On-Site

1. **Talk to strangers**: Start with "Hi, where do you work?"
2. **Sit with new people at lunch**: The easiest networking method at GDC
3. **Use GDC Vault**: Missed sessions can be watched later — focus on live networking
4. **Leverage social media**: Connect in real-time via #GDC on X (Twitter)

### Must-Have Items

- Business cards (at least 100)
- Portable charger
- Meta Quest + charger
- Laptop
- Water bottle, snacks
